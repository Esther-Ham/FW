My Skills.

Painting

I love the flexibility of being able to work with any medium and style I want. The beauty of art is that mistakes are okay to make and can sometimes turn into happy accidents. You don't have to have masterful ideas to begin, but a willingness to try and fail multiple times. You have to be able to learn from past mistakes and learn new skills from master artists. It's an avenue of self expression that allows me to communicate with the world without saying a single word.

Coding

Coding is a lot like art, in that mistakes can be made and corrected. You can play around with different elements that can shape the way your website will look, work, and function. You can learn from other skilled coders and there are endless opportunities to hone your skills. To be a coder means to be a lifelong learner as the world continues to move forward and as technology continues to develop for the better.
